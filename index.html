Frequently Asked Questions

How is the monthly payment calculated?

We use standard amortization formulas used by major US lenders. We calculate Principal & Interest based on your rate and term, then add estimated Property Taxes, Home Insurance, and HOA fees to give you a complete "PITI" estimate.

Is my data private?

Yes. All calculations happen locally on your device. We do not store your income, credit score, or home price history on our servers. If you choose to "Contact a Banker," only the information you explicitly submit in that form is transmitted securely.

How do I delete my data?

Since we do not create user accounts or store calculation history in the cloud, simply uninstalling the app will remove all local data from your device.
